/ WHAT WE FIX

Multi-service repair.
One shop you can trust.

⚙️
Oil changes
Routine maintenance done right. Quick turnaround, no pressure.
Skip this long enough and your engine can seize. That's a $6,000–$10,000 repair — versus a $50 oil change. It's the easiest thing to stay on top of.
🔧
Brakes
Inspection, pads, rotors, calipers, fluid flush. Diagnosed before quoted.
Grinding, squealing, or a car that pulls when you stop aren't just annoyances — they mean your stopping distance is already longer than it should be. This is a safety issue, not a "get to it later" one.
💡
Engine diagnostics
Check engine light, performance issues — explained in plain language.
That light could mean a $20 sensor or the start of a $4,000 problem. Ignoring it doesn't make it smaller — it makes it more expensive. We'll tell you exactly what it means.
❄️
A/C & heating
Recharge, leak diagnosis, compressor service. Critical in Florida heat.
In a St. Augustine summer, a broken A/C isn't just uncomfortable — it's a real health risk, especially with kids in the car. If it's not blowing cold, don't wait.
🛞
Tires & rotation
Mount, balance, rotate, patch. All makes and models.
Worn or uneven tires are a leading cause of blowouts and hydroplaning. If your car vibrates at highway speed or pulls to one side, your tires are telling you something.
🚗
Suspension & alignment
Shocks, struts, steering, alignment. Your car should drive like it should.
If your car drifts, bounces hard over bumps, or your steering wheel sits crooked — your tires are wearing unevenly right now, and your handling in an emergency is already compromised.
⚡
Electrical
Alternators, starters, batteries, wiring. Diagnosed, explained, fixed.
A failing battery or alternator doesn't just leave you stranded — it can corrupt the computer systems modern cars depend on. Strange warning lights, slow starts, or flickering electronics are early warnings worth catching.
🔩
Engine & transmission
Major repair and rebuilds — quoted honestly before anything starts.
These are the two most expensive systems in your car. A small leak or odd noise caught early can be a $300 fix. Left alone, the same problem becomes a $5,000 rebuild. We'll tell you where you stand — no pressure.
🔍
Inspections & tune-ups
Pre-purchase checks, belts, filters, fluids. Know what you're buying.
Buying a used car without an inspection is a gamble. And routine tune-ups catch the small stuff — a cracked belt, low fluid — before it leaves you stranded on the side of the road.
